This site is my starting point for learning in public.

I want it to become a place where I can record the real path toward AI Infra engineering: the clean concepts, the broken environments, the benchmark surprises, and the small experiments that slowly become reliable systems knowledge.

## What I will write about

- CUDA and GPU learning notes
- vLLM and LLM deployment experiments
- Linux / Ubuntu environment debugging
- Inference benchmarking and profiling
- Project logs that are honest about tradeoffs

## The tone

I do not want this blog to feel like a cold engineering manual. I want it to be technical, but still relaxed; personal, but not noisy; useful, but not pretending that every post is final truth.

If a note helps one future reader avoid a painful setup bug, it is already worth publishing.
